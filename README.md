## 1. Executive Summary
This report summarizes an e-commerce performance analysis conducted on a synthetic dataset from 2023 to early 2026. The analysis focused on revenue trends, customer behavior, churn patterns, product performance, and geographic sales distribution. Overall, the business shows recovery in 2025 with improved customer retention, but faces risks from declining average order value and high revenue dependence on a small group of customers.
## 2. Project Objectives
  - Evaluate whether the business is profitable and growing
  - Analyze customer buying behavior and activity trends
  - Assess customer value and churn patterns
  - Identify top and low-performing products
  - Determine regional revenue distribution
## 3. Data and Tools
The dataset was synthetically generated using AI and analyzed using Microsoft Excel, Power Query, Power Pivot, and DAX. Data cleaning, transformation, and modeling were performed, and a calendar table was created for time-series analysis.
